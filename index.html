Entreprenurial
BrickIt
BrickIt is a website I created that takes an image and turns it into a 2d mosaic you can build out of LEGO bricks. Not only will it make your image look like a LEGO mosaic, it will give you the instruction manual and parts list so you can build it yourselft. The site has been an ongoing project for several years and has recently been featured in Gizmodo, TheBrickBlogger, and other sites. The site makes money off of affialte marketing currently.
BookDump
Co-founded with two other ISU students, BookDump is a textbook buyback company with a focus on student convience. Students upload their ISBN's to the site I created from scratch to recieve a cash quote for their books. We hire "BookDump Ambassadors" each semester to meet our customers all over campus and pay customers cash on the spot. We make our money selling the books back online in bulk. We hope to be at several campuses soon.
